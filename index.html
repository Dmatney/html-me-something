Labbits that helped shape my relationship
What is a Labbit?

A labbit is a pop art icon by Frank Kozik. They are rabbit like creatures often with mustaches, a tail, a butthole, and no visible mouth. They come in a variety of forms. You can find a variety of forms:

  • Vinyl Collectables
  • Key Chains
  • Plush dongles
  • Plushes ranging from tiny to huge
  • Chairs
    • Clothing
  • and more!
My wife and I have collected them since the beginning of our relationship. We currently have around two dozen. They have been there for all the big parts of our life together.Including our wedding!

The VIL: Very Important Labbits

Lustmort Von Pinkerton

Lustmort is the first and some may argue greatest labbit. On my wife and I's third date at a Barnes and Noble his noble cowprint pattern caught my eye so I brought him to show my then girlfriend. She fellow in love with him due to his soft fur, slightly twisted gait, and his buthole. His personality is one who must be on the top of the food chain. Bullying those around him and ensuring he gets the best of the best. Despite this he sparked a love for the strange fluffy creatures and even ended up guarding the guest book for our rehersal dinner and for our wedding. He is also the high priest of the labbit gods "Alpha" and "Omega"

Buffy Muffy

With a comforting beard, Buffy Muffy is more distinguished than the common labbit. He is also more caring. While initial bought as a gift, he stayed with me after I broke my arm. Being a medium size labbit he was the perfect height to prop up a broken arm. He is not only a healer to me, others have benefited from his caring nature

Omega

Omega is the largest type of labbit. God Teir. He was a gift to congratulate my wife on passing her medical exams. The other labbits were quick to worship him

Alpha

Alpha is wife of Omega. She was given to me by my wife to comfort me when my brother was dying. She like Omega is gloriously huge! However she gave birth to demons called littons
